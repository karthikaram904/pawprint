Frequently Asked Questions

01
How long is a typical pet photography session?

Sessions can range from 30 minutes to 3 hours depending on your chosen package. We make sure your pet stays comfortable and happy throughout.

02
Can I include my pet’s favorite toys or props?

Absolutely! We encourage bringing your pet’s favorite toys, blankets, or props to make them feel at home and capture their personality.

03
Do you shoot indoors or outdoors?

We offer both indoor studio sessions with professional lighting and outdoor sessions in parks or your preferred locations for natural shots.

04
Can I book a video session for my pet?

Yes! We provide short cinematic videos of your pet, perfect for sharing memories with family and social media.

05
How long does it take to receive the edited photos?

Edited photos are usually delivered within 5–7 business days. For larger packages, delivery may take up to 10 days, depending on the number of images.

06
Do you provide printed albums or just digital files?

We offer both. Premium packages include beautifully designed photo albums, but all sessions come with high-resolution digital images for easy sharing.
